7 Road Hazard Detectors

Black ice, bridge freeze, fog, flood, diesel gel, high wind, mudslide.

▼
Real-time alerts calculated from live weather data before you reach the hazard. Each detector uses its own threshold model — bridge freeze triggers at different temps than black ice, diesel gel is tracked against your fuel type. All 7 run simultaneously on every query.
Live Now
📡

DOT / 511 Live Feeds

Live data from all 50 states. Construction, closures, accidents.

▼
CleanShot pulls directly from state DOT 511 feeds — the same data source dispatchers use. Incidents, construction zones, road closures, and weather advisories from every state transportation department. Updated in real time, not cached hours-old data.
Live Now
⏱️

HOS Guardian

FMCSA 11/14/70-hour advisory tracking with smart alerts.

▼
Tracks your available drive time against FMCSA 11-hour driving, 14-hour on-duty, and 70-hour/8-day limits simultaneously. Alerts you before you hit a limit — not after. Never get caught off guard by an inspection.
Live Now
🅿️

Smart Parking Runway

HOS-aware parking suggestions based on how far you can still drive.

▼
Combines your remaining HOS with your current location to calculate your parking window — how far you can go before you legally need to stop. Shows truck stops and rest areas within that range so you're never scrambling at the last minute.
Live Now
📢

CB-Style Voice Alerts

Text-to-speech with beep + flash on critical hazards.

▼
High-severity alerts are announced out loud in a CB radio style so you don't have to look at the screen. 5 severity levels — only the most critical ones trigger audio. Flash + beep on the highest severity so nothing gets missed even in a loud cab.
Live Now

English + Spanish

Full bilingual support. Switch on the fly.

▼
Every alert, label, and menu is available in both English and Spanish. Switch languages at any time without restarting the app. Voice alerts are also spoken in your selected language.

Built for 2G

Kilobytes per query. Works in dead zones other apps won't.

▼
Typical weather apps burn 50–200 MB/month in background data — even when you're not using them. CleanShot pulls data only when you ask, using kilobytes per query. Built to work on capped data plans and in low-signal areas where other apps give up.
